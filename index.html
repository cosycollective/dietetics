Meet Jenna

she/her

BFSc&Nutr, MNutr&Dietet, APD

Jenna is a Senior Dietitian who is passionate about providing evidence-based nutrition care that is flexible and individualised to the needs of her patients. She is a compassionate and friendly healthcare provider with over a decade of clinical nutrition experience having worked as a dietitian in both public and private hospitals in rural and metropolitan locations. Jenna has worked across all healthcare settings from intensive care to acute hospital wards to rehabilitation with Alfred Health in Victoria. She has also worked in many specialised outpatient services including clinics in cardiology, diabetes, renal disease, food allergy, neurology, and home enteral nutrition.

Jenna has extensive experience working in intensive care, neurosurgery, neurology, and stroke at The Alfred Hospital where she also recently set up the nutrition service in the multidisciplinary Idiopathic Intracranial Hypertension outpatient clinic, providing nutrition support to patients on a range of medical therapies, including GLP-1 weight loss drugs. Jenna has also completed nutrition research and published work in medical journals with a focus on understanding and improving nutrition intake and care throughout hospitalisation. She is passionate about bridging the gap between hospital care and life at home after hospital discharge.

Jenna is excited to work with you in providing a treatment plan that is goal-orientated, effective and achievable.

Services

Nutrition for a range of medical conditions and lifestyle concerns

Jenna provides advice and expertise in nutrition therapy for medical conditions, including but not limited to:

  • Supporting patients on GLP-1 weight loss drugs for management of type 2 diabetes, obesity and other clinical conditions
  • Very Low Energy Diets (VLEDs) for a range of medical reasons, including safe and sustainable transition back to normalised eating
  • Lifestyle or chronic diseases such as type 2 diabetes, pre-diabetes, and cardiovascular disease
  • Digestive issues such as IBS, constipation, diarrhoea or food sensitivities
  • Food allergies or Coeliac Disease

Neurology nutrition

Jenna provides specialised services and expertise in the nutritional management of neurological conditions, including:

  • Idiopathic Intracranial Hypertension (IIH)
  • Enteral tube feeding plans or oral nutrition support following stroke or neurosurgical interventions
  • Neurodegenerative conditions such as Multiple Sclerosis (MS), Parkinson's disease, or Motor Neuron Disease (MND)

Nutrition support following hospitalisation

Jenna provides specialised services and expertise in nutrition care following hospitalisation, including:

  • Dietary counselling to treat hospital-acquired and/or disease-related malnutrition
  • Oral or enteral nutrition support for patients following hospital discharge

If you don't see your medical condition above or are wondering whether Jenna can help you navigate your specific situation through nutrition care, get in touch.

Cancellation of appointment

If you need to cancel your appointment, please do so prior to 48 hours before the appointment time to avoid paying a cancellation fee.

Please note that if you do not attend or cancel within 48 hours of a scheduled appointment, you will be charged 50% of the appointment fee.

Funding

NDIS

National Disability Insurance Scheme

Jenna is able to see NDIS participants whose funding is either self-managed or plan-managed only. Fee assistance depends on your NDIS plan and goals.

Note: Jenna does not provide dietetic services to agency-managed NDIS participants.

Medicare rebates

You may be eligible for a care plan through your GP in which Medicare will partially cover up to 5 dietitian appointments per year. Please speak to your GP to find out if you are eligible for this prior to your appointment with Jenna. Once payment is made in full, you will be provided with an invoice so you can claim the rebate through Medicare.

Private health insurance

If you have ‘extras’ including Dietitian cover, rebates will be available. The exact amount will depend on your cover type. Please speak to your insurer if you are unsure of your level of cover. Once payment is made in full, you will be provided with an invoice so you can make a claim with your insurer.
